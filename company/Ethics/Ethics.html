Ethics management

Our company strives to grow and develop with all stakeholders, including company board members and shareholders, by improving corporate value through more progressive and efficient movements for global management,

and to become a company that faithfully fulfills its obligations and responsibilities in trust with customers.

    1.

    Postures toward shareholders and investors.

  • VAEX protects shareholders' rights and respects shareholders' legitimate demands and proposals.
  • VAEX faithfully discloses management information to build mutual trust with shareholders and investors and strives to maximize shareholder profits.
    2.

    The attitude toward customers

  • VAEX will always think and act from the customer's point of view and fulfill its obligation to provide the best trusted service to protect customers' precious assets.
    3.

    The attitude toward competitors and partners.

  • VAEX complies with fair trade laws and respects the fair and free market economy order in accordance with the principle of free competition.
  • VAEX pursues joint development by establishing mutual trust and cooperative relationships through fair transactions with partners.
    4.

    Postures toward shareholders and investors.

  • Executives and employees of VAEX do not use internal information acquired for personal purposes.
  • Executives and employees of VAEX do not provide undisclosed material information that may affect VAEX's transactions to third parties without due process.
  • Executives and employees of VAEX must protect the company's physical property, intellectual property rights, trade secrets, etc., and do not use them for private purposes.
  • All executives and employees of VAEX must faithfully comply with the Code of Ethics, and if they violate it, they are responsible for the acts.
