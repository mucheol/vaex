Did you forget your password?

1. Click the Forgot password on the login screen.
2. After selecting and entering a phone or email, security verification.
3. Enter the code.
  • If you enter the code sent to email or phone within 90 seconds, the password will be reset.
  • Please be careful that coin withdrawal will be suspended for 48 hours when the password is reset.
