Can I only see the market information of the coin we want?

1. Choose the transaction you want.
2. Coin name to add to [favorites], Click on the star on the left.
  • When clicked, the color is filled and added to favorites.
3. Favorite list
  • You can remove from the favorites list by clicking the filled star again.
4. [Favorites] on the trading screen
  • You can also check the Favorite list on the Trading screen and add and remove it.
  • You can also view only the coin names of the favorites list by clicking the icon on the right side of the search window.
