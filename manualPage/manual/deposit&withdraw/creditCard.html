How do I charge with my credit card?

1. Please select Buy Crypto -> Credit Card in the upper right corner of the main screen.
2. Fill out the form.
  • Charging is possible only when it exceeds the minimum unit that appears when entering the form.
3. Enter your wallet address.
4. Enter your email.
5. Email verification.
6. Enter basic information details.
7. Enter your billing address.
8. Card billing address.
9. Enter the card information.
10. Payment
  • Please check the terms and conditions and follow the credit card company's instructions to make the payment.
  • After the first payment, the procedure will be reduced.
