How do I verify my ID verification?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click [Verification] in the ID Verification column
3. Select your country or region
4. Certified ID Type Choose
5. Fill out the form
  • ID NO. is compared and checked with the picture to be uploaded, so please fill it out accurately.
6. Upload pictures
  • Please take a picture and upload it like the image on the right.
  • Please read all the precautions below the screen.
  • When the upload is complete, press the Submit button to complete it.
7. Waiting for the judgement
  • The verification number, ID card, and image entered in the previous step are compared to be reviewed.
