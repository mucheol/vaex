How do I change my nickname?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click [Change]
3. Enter the nickname you want and click the confirm button.
  • Nicknames cannot be duplicated.
4. Complete the change of nickname.
  • You can change your nickname without limit to the number of times.
