How do I change and register my email?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click [Change] in the email box.
  • For mobile users, please press [Connect].
3. If you login with your email, Google verification and mobile authentication are required.
  • You must complete at least one authentication of the Google authenticator or mobile authentication to change your email.
4. Fill out the form (login with your email)
  • Please authenticate the email with the existing email and the code sent to the email you want to change.
  • Enter the code of Google Authentication and click the change button to complete the change.
5. Fill out the form (when you login your phone)
  • Please authenticate the email with the code sent to the email you want to register.
  • In the case of mobile login, additional mobile authentication is required when modifying the email after registering the email.
  • Enter the code of Google Authentication and click the change button to complete the change.
