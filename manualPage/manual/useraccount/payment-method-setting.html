How do I set up my payment method?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click P2P settings
3. Payment Management
  • Click [Settings] in the Payment Method setting box.
4. If you don't have enough authentication,
  • All four authentication is required for payment management setting.
  • If it's already been certified, we'll move on to item 5.
5. Form of payment method
  • The forms of Alipay and WeChat are the same.
  • If it's already been certified, we'll move on to item 4.
6. Payment method setting completed
  • You can change and delete the payment method that you registered once.
  • If it's not an accurate information, it can be deleted automatically.
