How do I register my mobile number?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click [Connect] in the Phone number box.
3. Fill out the form
  • Enter your mobile authentication code, Google Authentication code, and click Connect to complete your mobile number registration.
  • Phones that have already been registered cannot be registered in duplicate.
