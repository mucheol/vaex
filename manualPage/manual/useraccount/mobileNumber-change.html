How do I register or cancel my mobile number?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click Change or Turn off in the phone number column
  • If you want to change it to a different number, [Change].
  • If you want disconnection, please select [Turn off].
3. When you change your mobile number
  • If you receive the code from the existing and new phones, authenticate them, and write down the Google Authentication number, the change will be completed.
4. When you turn off the mobile connection
  • Enter the mobile authentication, Google Authentication code, and click Submit to disconnect the phone.
