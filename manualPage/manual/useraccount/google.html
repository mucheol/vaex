How do I register for Google authentication?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen.
2. Click Connect in the Google Authenticator column
3. App Download
4. After scanning the QR code, add a verification token.
5. Complete linking
  • Please complete the authentication with the numbers that appear in the VAEX password and Google Authenticator.
