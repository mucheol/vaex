How do I change my password?

1. Click the human icon at the top right of the main screen
2. Click [Change] in the password box
3. For email login, Google verification or phone verification is required.
  • You must complete at least one authentication of Google Authentication or mobile authentication to change your password.
4. Write it according to the password change form.
  • After filling out the form, click “Click to send” to receive the code and enter it.
  • Coin withdrawal is not possible for 48 hours after changing the password, so please be careful.
